    Not All Probiotics Are Created Equal

    ProBiora is different from other oral probiotics because it contains ProBiora3® — a patented blend of three beneficial strains naturally found in the mouth. These probiotics are designed to work where oral health starts: on teeth and below the gums.

    By populating the mouth with beneficial bacteria, ProBiora helps crowd out harmful bacteria, rebalance oral microbiome balance, and support healthier teeth and gums.

    Most competing products use strains native to the gut, not the oral environment — making them far less effective at actually colonizing the mouth. ProBiora's mouth-specific strains are uniquely suited to thrive where you need them most.
